# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-27

Polymarket Strategy Assistant ("the Extension") provides real-time market viewing and analysis assistance for Polymarket pages.

## What Data We Collect
We do not collect personal data.

The Extension does not collect or transmit:
- Name, email, phone number, address, or account credentials
- Payment data or financial account credentials
- Personal communications
- Health data

## What Data Is Processed
The Extension processes:
- Public market data fetched from external APIs (read-only)
- Public content visible on `polymarket.com` pages for on-page analysis and UI rendering

## Local Storage
The current version does not require local user-data storage for runtime features.

## External Services
The Extension may request public data from:
- `https://gamma-api.polymarket.com/*`
- `https://api.coingecko.com/*`

These requests are used only to retrieve public market data.

## Data Sharing and Sale
We do not sell personal data.
We do not share personal data with third parties because we do not collect personal data.

## Security
The Extension minimizes privileges and does not execute remote executable code.
All executable scripts are packaged with the extension.

## Changes to This Policy
If this policy changes, the updated version will be published with a new "Last updated" date.
